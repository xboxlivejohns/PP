Frequently asked questions

No tap water is required for standard services. The van carries 0 TDS pure water and is set up as a self-contained mobile detailing unit. If a specialist service ever needs electricity, this can be confirmed during booking.

0 TDS pure water has had dissolved minerals removed, meaning it dries without leaving mineral deposits. That helps achieve a completely spot-free finish on paint, glass, trims, and wheels.
